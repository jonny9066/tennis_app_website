The Android Tennis App

Tennis stats tracking on Android

Goal

Bring tennis game tracking to Android, with smooth perfromance and excellent user experience.

Accomplished objectives

  • Tennis ball tracking in real-time, on-device.
  • Bounce detection.
  • Mapping of court and tennis ball bounces to a minimap.
  • Manual selection of court coordinates.
  • Tested on Galaxy S23, with the Snapdragon Gen 2 platform (2023).

    • Main Challenges

    Tracking a tennis ball moving at high speed, ignoring static tennis balls.

    Real-time performance on mobile devices with limited resources and tendency to overheat.

    Collection and annotation of real-world data. Optimizing models for different court types, angles, and lighting.

    Planned features

  • Automatic detection of the court.
  • Ball detection quality improvmeents.
  • Performance optimization up to 30 fps.
  • Tracking of the tennis court in case of camera movement.
  • 'Fun mode' with visual effects like fire following ball, explosions on bounce.

    • Tennis ball tracking on iPhone

    SwingVision is an iPhone app built for tracking tennis. A main motivation behind this project is the desire to bring a similar experience to the Android platform. Although attempts by the SwingVision team have been made, no Android version has been released.
